Intrested in volunteering?

The BSPCA and BARC would love help from volunteers, all the shelters were founded by volunteers and thus they are always welcome to help.
In the mentioned shelters, you can volunteer to walk the dogs, groom the cats and play with all the pets, they also accept office volunteering, meaning that if you are good at office work and ready to lend a hand they will gladly accept you.
volunteering days at BSPCA are from Thursday- Saturday 9 AM- 1 PM.

Thrift Shops

What's better than shopping? shopping for a cause! visit the thriftshops to check out intresting items.
the thriftshops are the income of these shelters, they do not take any profit and the workers there are volunteers, do not forget that the shelters have no other way of income than the kindness of people. the thriftshops available are BSPCA Thrift Shop, Abu Saiba and the Dogfather Animal Rescue Charity Shop, 403 road 2413, Avenue 23, Saar.

Foster

If you are not sure if you could handle a pet and you don't know the responsibilities, you might consider fostering. But you first need to know what fostering is. Fostering means to get a pet from a rescue group or a shelter, to take care of that pet and keep it with you for a while until we find it a forever home. what is the purpose of fostering? well, fostering helps us make more space for New rescued pets to come, and it will for sure help the pets get used to what their lives would look like in the future. who knows? you might adopt the pet that you are fostering
